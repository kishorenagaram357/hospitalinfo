Yashodha Hospitals

Yashoda Hospitals is a chain of hospitals based in Hyderabad, India. It has branches in Somajiguda, Secunderabad, and Malakpet[1], with another branch coming up in Hitec City.[2] All of its branches are NABH[3] and NABL[4] accredited.

It was recognised as a leading hospital for oncology in 2015 after becoming the first hospital to achieve the milestone of treating 10,000 patients using RapidArc technology.[5]
