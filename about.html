Kamineni Hospitals

Kamineni Institute of Medical Sciences is a private medical college associated with a 1050 bed teaching Hospital located in Narketpally village, Nalgonda District, Telangana state of India on NH65 about 80 kilometers (50 miles) from the city of Hyderabad. The medical college hospital serves rural population in and around Narketpally
